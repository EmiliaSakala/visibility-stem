A BIT ABOUT US


Our goal is to change the narrative & highlight
the imprtant contributions that we Africans are
making in STEM


By sharing our stories with the world, our aim is
to create more opportunities for, and inspire the
next generation of, Africans in STEM.


Learn more about what we do and why we do it
below.

WHY STEM? WHY VSA?

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematices) fields play
an integral role in our day -to-day lives and pervade every facet of modern day
society.


Until now, there have been very few efforts to amplify African voices and
showcase the significant efforts being made across the content and by the
Diaspora.


Our platform aims to foster collaboration, facillitate the creation of outreach
projects and most importantly, to create a visible and realistic path for the
next generation of young Africans who want to pursue STEM careers.

MEET OUR TEAM

Directors

Nathasia Muwanigwa, Co-Founder

Nathasia is a highly motivated young woman currently
pursuing a PhD in neurobiology at the University of
Luxembourg. she hails from Harare, the capital of
Zimbabwe.


As of Aprial 2019, she is a PhD researcher at the
Luxembourg Center for Systems Biomedicine, in
Luxembourg. Her research focuses on stem cell based
modelling of Parkinson's disease. She is a dedicated
advocate for underrepresented individuals in STEM.
